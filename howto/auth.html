About the Site Author

About Kelvin

My name is Kelvin Watson and I live in Portland, Oregon. I was born and raised in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada. I obtained my Bachelor's Science in the Pharmaceutical Sciences at the University of British Columbia (UBC) in Vancouver, Canada in 2007. Prior to obtaining my B.Sc(Pharm.), I completed third year in the Microbiology and Immunology program, also at UBC. As such, my educational background has mostly been in the life and health sciences.

I decided to pursue the professional post-baccalaureate Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science at Oregon State University for a number of reasons.

After almost seven years as a pharmacist, I decided it was time for a change. I left my position, and moved to the United States with my spouse in 2013. After some time off, I began to realize that I had largely ignored a passion of mine, computer science. Even during my years as a pharmacist, I often found myself more interested in the technical aspects of my profession, including automated dispensing, robotics, and various aspects of healthcare software.

Another significant reason why I have chosen this field of study is my husband. He is a software engineer who absolutely loves his career path, and he is a wonderful source of encouragement and inspiration.

I enjoy Android development, but also programming in C, C++, Python, JavaScript, and PHP. I have found computer science to stimulate a more systematic way of thinking and approach to problem solving that I had not experienced in my formal education in the past. After completing this degree, I hope to pursue a career in either software development, or computational drug discovery, or both, realizing that neither are necessarily mutually exclusive.

My interests beyond data structures and algorithms include writing and producing my own music. I also am familiar with some digital music editing tools as one of my passions is writing and producing my own music. I am not an extremely active person, but I do enjoy weightlifting on my spare time as I feel it helps clear my mind. I live a simple lifestyle, and enjoy things like having coffee, visiting wineries, or having a meal outside in the sun.
